Meet Our Founder

James Kimutai Cherono

Mediator at Compleat Consultants Africa

Kimutai is a Mediator & Advocate of the High Court of Kenya with a wide range of experience in advising people and creating innovative solutions to complex legal, social and commercial situations.

Kimutai assists parties at an impasse in their attempts to resolve disputes with familial or commercial partners, yet find the prospect of litigation both time consuming, expensive and ruinous to otherwise amicable, profitable partnerships.

AFFILIATIONS: Strathmore Dispute Resolution Centre, WasilianaHub Mediators, The Federation of Women Lawyers (FIDA) Kenya, Law Society of Kenya.
